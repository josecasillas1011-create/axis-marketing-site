The leak

The phone rings while your team is busy.
The lead does not wait.

For local service businesses, missed calls usually happen at the worst possible time: during jobs, after hours, on weekends, or when the front desk is helping someone else.

After-hours calls

A caller needs help when your office is closed and voicemail is the only thing standing between them and another provider.

On-the-job missed calls

The owner or crew cannot stop mid-job to answer, so the lead never gets a real first response.

Urgent service requests

When the issue feels urgent, the first business to respond often earns the next conversation.

How it works

A simple front-desk layer
for the calls you miss.

Start with the part that matters most: answer the call, capture the details, and route the handoff cleanly.

Caller reaches your business

Forward missed calls, after-hours calls, or a dedicated tracking number to the AI Front Desk.

The agent handles the conversation

It asks one question at a time and captures name, phone, job details, location, urgency, and common FAQs.

Your team gets the handoff

You receive the summary and call back, schedule, or follow up based on the caller's urgency.

Phase 1 stays intentionally simple. Deeper CRM, calendar, and SMS automations can be added after the call-capture offer is proven with real prospects.
