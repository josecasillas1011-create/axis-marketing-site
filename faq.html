GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) are the emerging discipline of getting your brand cited and recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot. When someone asks an AI "who's the best dental practice in Riverside?" — you want to be the answer it gives. Traditional SEO gets you ranked on Google's blue links. GEO/AEO gets you into AI-generated answers, which are increasingly where high-intent searchers are looking. It's not replacing SEO yet, but businesses that start building AI authority now will have a significant advantage in 12–18 months. We offer it as a standalone service or bundled with SEO.
